Odisha Coffee is more than just a brand – it's a journey that starts from the rich, red soil of Odisha’s tribal highlands and ends in the warm cup in your hands. Established in 2025, our mission is to bring the pure, earthy essence of Odisha’s coffee to the world. We work closely with local farmers to ethically source the finest Arabica, Robusta, and rare indigenous coffee beans. Our passion lies in preserving traditional methods while embracing innovation in roasting, brewing, and packaging. Whether you visit our café, explore our products, or enjoy a cup at home – you're not just tasting coffee, you're experiencing a story of culture, sustainability, and community.
