FAQs - Odisha Coffee Company:-
What makes Odisha Coffee unique?

Odisha Coffee is grown in the Eastern Ghats with a distinct flavor profile, cultivated sustainably by local farmers.

Where do you source your coffee beans?

We source our beans directly from tribal farmers in Koraput and neighboring regions of Odisha.

Do you offer organic coffee?

Yes, all our coffee is 100% organic, free from pesticides and processed naturally.

Can I visit your coffee plantation?

Yes, we offer guided tours of our plantations with prior appointment. Please contact us for details.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently, we ship across India. International shipping is coming soon.
